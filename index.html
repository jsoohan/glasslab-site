Method

Three layers: clinic menu, generic protocol, PubMed evidence.

  • 1 11 Korean clinics scraped — 4516 procedure listings, 90.9% mapped to 48 generic MoAs.
  • 2 Brands kept in brand_to_class_map, never in PubMed queries.
  • 3 PubMed query = ({MoA MeSH + TIAB synonyms}) AND ({Indication MeSH + TIAB synonyms}).
  • 4 Tier label per paper: tier1 (RCT / meta / SR), tier2 (CT / observational), tier3 (case), tier0 (review).
