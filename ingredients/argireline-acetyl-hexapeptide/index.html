The anti-wrinkle efficacy of argireline, a synthetic hexapeptide, in Chinese subjects: a randomized, placebo-controlled study
PMID: 23417317 · RCT · positive · topical
Topical argireline (acetyl hexapeptide) applied twice daily for 4 weeks demonstrated significant anti-wrinkle efficacy of 48.9% in Chinese subjects versus 0% in the placebo group, with statistically significant reductions in objective skin roughness parameters.
The efficacy study of the combination of tripeptide-10-citrulline and acetyl hexapeptide-3. A prospective, randomized controlled study
PMID: 28150423 · RCT · positive · topical
Acetyl hexapeptide-3 demonstrated confirmed antiwrinkle activity and significant reduction in TEWL, while tripeptide-10 citrulline also showed antiwrinkle efficacy, though the synergistic mechanism between the two peptides remained unclear.
Pilot study of topical acetyl hexapeptide-8 in the treatment for blepharospasm in patients receiving botulinum toxin therapy
PMID: 23146065 · RCT · positive · topical
Topical acetyl hexapeptide-8 was safe and showed a promising trend for extending the duration of botulinum toxin therapy in blepharospasm patients, with active group maintaining symptom control for 3.7 months versus 3.0 months for placebo.
