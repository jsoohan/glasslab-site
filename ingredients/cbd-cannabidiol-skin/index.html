Topical nanoencapsulated cannabidiol cream as an innovative strategy combating UV-A-induced nuclear and mitochondrial DNA injury: A pilot randomized clinical study
PMID: 39025264 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
Topically applied nanoencapsulated CBD cream significantly reduced UV-A-induced epidermal hyperplasia, nuclear DNA base lesions (8-oxoguanine), and mitochondrial DNA deletions (ND4 and ND1) associated with photoaging compared to vehicle control.
Efficacy and safety of cannabidiol oil in psoriasis: a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial
PMID: 41459647 · RCT · mixed · oral
Oral CBD oil 60mg/day did not significantly improve PASI scores in psoriasis patients, though temporary improvements in itch relief and sleep onset latency were observed.
The neural and psychophysiological effects of cannabidiol in youth with alcohol use disorder: A randomized controlled clinical trial
PMID: 40500407 · RCT · negative · oral
Acute oral administration of 600mg CBD showed no significant effects on neurometabolic, neurobehavioral, psychophysiological, or alcohol use outcomes in youth with alcohol use disorder compared to placebo.
Tolerability profile of topical cannabidiol and palmitoylethanolamide: a compilation of single-centre randomized evaluator-blinded clinical and in vitro studies in normal skin
PMID: 34022073 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
Topical formulations containing CBD and PEA were found to be non-irritating and non-sensitizing in healthy adults, with only mild phototoxicity observed for a hemp seed oil product at 48 hours.
A Randomized Controlled Trial of Topical Cannabidiol for the Treatment of Thumb Basal Joint Arthritis
PMID: 35637038 · RCT · positive · topical
Topical CBD (6.2 mg/mL in shea butter) applied twice daily for 2 weeks significantly improved pain scores, disability measures, grip and pinch strength in thumb basal joint arthritis patients compared to shea butter control, with no adverse events.
A Randomized, Open-Label Trial to Assess Feasibility and Tolerability of Topical Cannabis Balms for the Treatment of Aromatase Inhibitor-Associated Musculoskeletal Syndrome (AIMSS)
PMID: 41467893 · RCT · positive · topical
Both CBD and THC topical balms applied three times daily were feasible and well-tolerated in breast cancer patients with AIMSS, with 86% reporting improvement in musculoskeletal symptoms and only minor skin irritation in 24% of participants.
Pharmacokinetics of cannabidiol administered by 3 delivery methods at 2 different dosages to healthy dogs
PMID: 30026641 · clinical_trial · mixed · mixed
Oral CBD-infused oil provided the most favorable pharmacokinetic profile with dose-proportional exposure, while transdermal cream showed lower systemic absorption compared to oral formulations in healthy dogs.
Cannabidiol enhances consolidation of explicit fear extinction in humans
PMID: 23307069 · RCT · positive · oral
CBD (32 mg) administered post-extinction enhanced consolidation of fear extinction learning in humans as measured by shock expectancy, suggesting potential as an adjunct to extinction-based therapies for anxiety disorders.
Novel cannabidiol aspartame combination treatment (JW-100) significantly reduces ISGA score in atopic dermatitis: Results from a randomized double-blinded placebo-controlled interventional study
PMID: 34056830 · RCT · positive · topical
A novel topical formulation of CBD combined with aspartame (JW-100) significantly reduced ISGA scores in atopic dermatitis patients over 14 days, with 50% achieving clear or almost clear skin versus 20% and 15% in CBD-only and placebo groups respectively.
An oil-in-water emulsion containing a combination of ginger extract and synthetic cannabidiol with potent in vitro anti-inflammatory effects alleviates symptoms of atopic dermatitis in a clinical tria
PMID: 39193679 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
An oil-in-water emulsion combining ginger extract and synthetic CBD (BNO 3731) significantly reduced atopic dermatitis symptoms in a 5-day clinical trial, decreasing itch intensity by 55% with no adverse events reported.
Distinct effects of {delta}9-tetrahydrocannabinol and cannabidiol on neural activation during emotional processing
PMID: 19124693 · RCT · mixed · oral
CBD (600mg oral) reduced anxiety and attenuated amygdala/cingulate cortex activation during fearful face processing, while THC increased anxiety and psychotic symptoms, demonstrating distinct neural mechanisms for each cannabinoid.
Cannabidiol reduces the anxiety induced by simulated public speaking in treatment-naïve social phobia patients
PMID: 21307846 · 2011 · RCT · positive · oral
A single oral dose of 600mg CBD significantly reduced anxiety, cognitive impairment, and discomfort during simulated public speaking in treatment-naïve social anxiety disorder patients compared to placebo.
Effects of Cannabidiol Ingestion on Thermoregulatory and Inflammatory Responses to Treadmill Exercise in the Heat in Recreationally Active Males
PMID: 39294920 · RCT · mixed · oral
Oral CBD ingestion (298mg) did not affect thermoregulation during exertional heat stress but showed minor immunosuppressive effects, reducing post-exercise CD14 and IL-6 inflammatory responses compared to placebo.
Pilot Study of Cannabidiol for Treatment of Aromatase Inhibitor-Associated Musculoskeletal Symptoms in Breast Cancer
PMID: 40751295 · clinical_trial · mixed · oral
Oral CBD (Epidiolex) at 100mg BID for 15 weeks was safe and tolerable, with 43.6% of breast cancer patients experiencing at least a 2-point reduction in worst joint pain associated with aromatase inhibitor therapy.
Safety and Tolerability of Transdermal Cannabidiol Gel in Children With Developmental and Epileptic Encephalopathies: A Nonrandomized Controlled Trial
PMID: 34477852 · 2021 · clinical_trial · positive · transdermal
CBD transdermal gel was safe and well tolerated in children with developmental and epileptic encephalopathies, associated with significant reductions in seizure frequency and improvements in quality of life measures, with mostly mild-to-moderate application-site adverse events.
