A cream containing microdoses of mandelic acid and Centella asiatica applied twice daily for 12 weeks significantly improved skin thickness by 5%, hydration from Day 7, and barrier function by 40% at Day 84 in patients aged 65+ with dermatoporosis, with excellent safety.
