Enhancing Facial Rejuvenation Outcomes With a Novel Retinaldehyde-Based Cream: A Comparative Randomized Intra-Individual Study
PMID: 41312593 · RCT · positive · topical
A cream containing retinaldehyde, niacinamide, and haritaki fruit extract significantly improved wrinkles, firmness, texture, skin tone, and radiance within 1 month when used as an adjunct to minimally-to-moderately invasive facial rejuvenation procedures (chemical peels, HA injections, or fractional laser) compared to untreated control hemiface.
The utilization of nonthermal blue (405-425 nm) and near infrared (850-890 nm) light in aesthetic dermatology and surgery-a multicenter study
PMID: 16414904 · clinical_trial · positive · LED
Non-thermal dual wavelength blue (405-420 nm) and near infrared (850-900 nm) light therapy reduced post-resurfacing erythema by 90%, improved skin radiance and pore size in 90% of patients, and smoothed fine wrinkles in 45% of patients with stable results at three months, outperforming glycolic peel and vitamin C controls.
Evaluation of the effectiveness and safety of peppermint peel (PMP) soft peeling for skin ageing
PMID: 32425029 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
Peppermint peel (PMP) soft peeling demonstrated 0% therapeutic failure rate and was effective and safe for treating signs of chrono and photo-aging across 121 subjects over 4 biweekly sessions, with only minimal flaking and slight redness as adverse events.
