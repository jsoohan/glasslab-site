분석된 논문 (21편)

The effect of dietary intake of coenzyme Q10 on skin parameters and condition: Results of a randomised, placebo-controlled, double-blind study
PMID: 27548886 · 2017 · RCT · mixed · oral
Oral supplementation with CoQ10 (50 and 150 mg daily for 12 weeks) significantly reduced wrinkles and microrelief lines and improved skin smoothness, while limiting seasonal deterioration of viscoelasticity, though no significant effects were observed on skin hydration, dermis thickness, or minimal erythema dose.
Coenzyme Q10 supplementation in burn patients: a double-blind placebo-controlled randomized clinical trial
PMID: 38431600 · RCT · mixed · oral
Oral CoQ10 supplementation (300 mg/day for 10 days) in burn patients had no significant effect on inflammatory markers or oxidative stress, but did significantly improve serum albumin levels compared to placebo.
Effects of a Combination of Water-Soluble CoenzymeQ10 and Collagen on Skin Parameters and Condition:Results of a Randomised, Placebo-Controlled,Double-Blind Study
PMID: 32120787 · RCT · mixed · oral
A 12-week oral supplement combining water-soluble CoQ10 and collagen improved dermis density, reduced periorbital wrinkle area and total wrinkle score, and improved skin smoothness, though hydration, dermis thickness, TEWL, and viscoelasticity showed no significant changes.
Effects of idebenone on color vision in patients with leber hereditary optic neuropathy
PMID: 23263355 · RCT · positive · oral
Idebenone (900 mg/day orally) improved tritan color vision compared to placebo in Leber hereditary optic neuropathy patients, with the most prominent effect in those at imminent risk of further vision loss.
Efficacy of food supplement to improve metabolic syndrome parameters in patients affected by moderate to severe psoriasis during anti-TNFα treatment
PMID: 24442048 · RCT · positive · oral
Addition of a nutraceutical supplement containing CoQ10, krill oil, lipoic acid, resveratrol, vitamin E, and selenium to etanercept therapy significantly improved HDL cholesterol and triglyceride levels in psoriasis patients with metabolic syndrome compared to etanercept alone.
An oral nutraceutical containing antioxidants, minerals and glycosaminoglycans improves skin roughness and fine wrinkles
PMID: 19570098 · RCT · positive · oral
An oral nutraceutical containing antioxidants, minerals, and glycosaminoglycans taken daily for 12 weeks resulted in a statistically significant 21.2% improvement in skin roughness and fine wrinkles compared to only 1.7% improvement in the placebo group.
A dietary supplement improves facial photoaging and skin sebum, hydration and tonicity modulating serum fibronectin, neutrophil elastase 2, hyaluronic acid and carbonylated proteins
PMID: 25732262 · RCT · positive · oral
Oral supplementation with a combination containing coenzyme Q10, Pycnogenol, collagen, hyaluronic acid, chondroitin sulfate, and glucosamine sulfate for 4 weeks significantly improved facial photoaging scores and skin sebum, hydration, and tonicity while modulating serum biomarkers including fibronectin, neutrophil elastase 2, hyaluronic acid, and carbonylated proteins compared to placebo.
Coenzyme Q(10), vitamin E, selenium, and methionine in the treatment of chronic recurrent viral mucocutaneous infections
PMID: 22079390 · clinical_trial · positive · oral
Oral supplementation with coenzyme Q10, vitamin E, selenium, and methionine combined with conventional antiviral therapies significantly accelerated healing and reduced relapse rates in patients with recurrent HPV warts, herpes simplex genitalis, and herpes zoster, associated with improved antioxidant capacity and enhanced antiviral immune markers.
Topical antioxidants protect the skin from chemical-induced irritation in the repetitive washing test: a placebo-controlled, double-blind study
PMID: 22624993 · RCT · positive · topical
A topical antioxidant cream demonstrated high radical scavenging activity and effectively protected skin from sodium lauryl sulfate-induced irritant contact dermatitis compared to cream base and a barrier cream, suggesting reactive oxygen species play a role in irritant contact dermatitis development.
Protective effect of anti-oxidants on endothelial function in young Korean-Asians compared to Caucasians
PMID: 22847195 · clinical_trial · positive · oral
Oral supplementation with antioxidants (vitamin C, vitamin E, and Coenzyme Q-10) for 14 days improved postprandial endothelial function (skin blood flow response) in young Korean-Asians who showed significant impairment after a high-fat meal, likely through free radical scavenging.
Identification of Genes Expressed in Hyperpigmented Skin Using Meta-Analysis of Microarray Data Sets
PMID: 25950827 · meta-analysis · neutral · unspecified
Meta-analysis of 5 microarray datasets identified 921 differentially expressed genes in hyperpigmented versus less pigmented skin, with TRIM63 and QPCT validated as novel markers potentially involved in regulating skin pigmentation physiology.
Hypo-collagenesis in photoaged skin predicts response to anti-aging cosmeceuticals
PMID: 23725304 · meta-analysis · mixed · topical
Subjects with hypo-collagenesis in photoaged skin responded to anti-aging cosmeceuticals (L-ascorbic acid, pentapeptide, α-lipoic acid, yeast extract, idebenone) 6.4 times more often than those with normal collagen levels, identifying baseline hypo-collagenesis as the sole predictor of treatment outcome.
Influence of nanostructured lipid carriers (NLC) on the physical properties of the Cutanova Nanorepair Q10 cream and the in vivo skin hydration effect
PMID: 20541000 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
Cutanova Nanorepair Q10 cream containing nanostructured lipid carriers (NLC) demonstrated significantly greater skin hydration improvement compared to an identical cream without NLC from day 28 onward, with superior subjective ratings for consistency, spreadability, and hydration.
Recombinant interferon alpha-2b and coenzyme Q10 as a postsurgical adjuvant therapy for melanoma: a 3-year trial with recombinant interferon-alpha and 5-year follow-up
PMID: 17505263 · clinical_trial · positive · oral
Long-term administration of recombinant interferon alpha-2b combined with oral coenzyme Q10 (400 mg/day) significantly decreased recurrence rates in stage I and II melanoma patients with negligible adverse effects over a 3-year treatment period.
Skin Antiageing and Systemic Redox Effects of Supplementation with Marine Collagen Peptides and Plant-Derived Antioxidants: A Single-Blind Case-Control Clinical Study
PMID: 26904164 · clinical_trial · positive · oral
Oral supplementation with marine collagen peptides combined with plant-derived antioxidants including coenzyme Q10 significantly improved skin elasticity, sebum production, and dermal ultrasonic markers without causing oxidative damage.
Polymorphisms in the Mitochondrial Genome Are Associated With Bullous Pemphigoid in Germans
PMID: 31824475 · other · neutral · unspecified
Mitochondrial DNA polymorphisms in the D-loop region and MT-ND4 gene were significantly associated with bullous pemphigoid susceptibility in Germans, but this study does not investigate coenzyme Q10 or any skin anti-aging intervention.
CoQ10 and endothelial function in Asians from Korea compared to Asians born in the United States and US born Caucasians
PMID: 23666274 · RCT · positive · oral
Two weeks of oral CoQ10 supplementation significantly improved endothelial function (measured via skin blood flow responses) in both US-born Caucasians and Asians, largely eliminating ethnic differences in vascular endothelial performance.
An Open Label Clinical Trial of a Multi-Ingredient Anti-Aging Moisturizer Designed to Improve the Appearance of Facial Skin
PMID: 26151786 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
A multi-ingredient anti-aging moisturizer containing coenzyme Q10 (ubiquinone) among other active ingredients produced statistically significant improvements in all assessed facial skin parameters including fine lines, wrinkles, clarity, roughness, and skin tone after 8 and 12 weeks of twice-daily use in 37 female subjects.
Effect of CoQ10 Administration to Psoriatic Iraqi Patients on Biological Therapy Upon Severity Index (PASI) and Quality of Life Index (DLQI) Before and After Therapy
PMID: 35848197 · RCT · positive · oral
Adding 100mg oral CoQ10 daily as adjuvant therapy to adalimumab (biological medication) in psoriatic patients for 12 weeks significantly improved both PASI scores (67.48% improvement) and quality of life (DLQI, 56.13% improvement) compared to adalimumab plus placebo.
An Open Label Clinical Trial to Evaluate the Efficacy and Tolerance of a Retinol and Vitamin C Facial Regimen in Women With Mild-to-Moderate Hyperpigmentation and Photodamaged Facial Skin
PMID: 27050703 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
A 12-week regimen combining 0.5% encapsulated retinol and a 30% vitamin C moisturizer containing coenzyme Q10 (ubiquinone) produced statistically significant improvements in hyperpigmentation and photodamage parameters, with transient dryness resolving by week 12.
Improved survival in patients with end-stage cancer treated with coenzyme Q(10) and other antioxidants: a pilot study
PMID: 20146896 · clinical_trial · positive · oral
End-stage cancer patients treated with oral coenzyme Q10 and other antioxidants survived a median of 17 months versus a predicted 12 months, representing over 40% longer survival than predicted.
