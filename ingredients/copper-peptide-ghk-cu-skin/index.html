Effects of topical copper tripeptide complex on CO2 laser-resurfaced skin
PMID: 16847171 · RCT · mixed · topical
GHK-Cu topical skin care products after CO2 laser resurfacing showed no statistically significant objective improvement in erythema resolution, wrinkles, or overall skin quality, but patient satisfaction was significantly higher in the GHK-Cu group (P=.04).
Evaluation of the effects of topical tripeptide-copper complex and zinc oxide on open-wound healing in rabbits
PMID: 17083573 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
Topical tripeptide-copper complex (GHK-Cu) significantly accelerated open wound healing in rabbits compared to zinc oxide and untreated controls, demonstrating superior wound contraction, faster granulation tissue formation, and enhanced neovascularization.
