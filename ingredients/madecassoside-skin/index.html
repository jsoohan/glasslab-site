분석된 논문 (4편)

Clinical, biometric and structural evaluation of the long-term effects of a topical treatment with ascorbic acid and madecassoside in photoaged human skin
PMID: 18503551 · RCT · positive · topical
Six months of topical application of 5% vitamin C and 0.1% madecassoside significantly improved clinical scores for wrinkles, suppleness, firmness, roughness, and hydration in photoaged skin, with structural remodelling of the elastic fibre network observed in two-thirds of subjects.
Evaluation of the Efficacy and Safety of a Panthenol-Enriched Mask for Skin Barrier Recovery After Facial Laser Treatment: Results of a Double-Blind Randomized Controlled Study
PMID: 40613435 · RCT · positive · topical
A panthenol-enriched mask containing madecassoside (centella asiatica extract) and bisabolol significantly reduced erythema, improved skin hydration, and accelerated skin barrier repair after non-ablative fractional laser treatment compared to saline dressing.
Randomized-Controlled Trial Comparing Safety and Efficacy of Repairing Balm vs Topical Antimicrobial for Post-Procedural Wounds
PMID: 40327581 · 2025 · RCT · positive · topical
A repairing balm containing madecassoside, panthenol, and metal salts demonstrated equivalent wound healing efficacy and safety to topical antibiotic ointment following cryotherapy for actinic keratoses, with no adverse events reported in either group.
A Split-Face Randomized Study on the Efficacy of a Platinum-Liposome-Based Facial Mask Containing Soothing Ingredients for Post-Photorejuvenation Skin Recovery
PMID: 40957858 · RCT · positive · topical
A platinum-liposome-based facial mask containing madecassoside and other soothing ingredients significantly improved skin hydration, reduced TEWL, and alleviated erythema, tightness, dryness, and scaliness compared to control following photorejuvenation, with synergistic anti-inflammatory effects demonstrated in vitro.
