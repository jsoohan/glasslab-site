A randomized, triple-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel study to evaluate the efficacy of a freshwater marine collagen on skin wrinkles and elasticity
PMID: 32799362 · RCT · positive · oral
Oral supplementation with hydrolyzed freshwater marine collagen (VWC) for 12 weeks produced a significant 35% reduction in wrinkle score and self-reported improvements in elasticity, hydration, radiance, and firmness compared to placebo in women aged 45-60.
Efficacy and tolerability of an oral supplement containing amino acids, iron, selenium, and marine hydrolyzed collagen in subjects with hair loss (androgenetic alopecia, AGA or FAGA or telogen effluvi
PMID: 37357646 · RCT · positive · oral
Oral supplementation with hydrolyzed fish-origin collagen combined with taurine, cysteine, methionine, iron, and selenium significantly improved clinical outcomes (GAS scores) in subjects with androgenetic alopecia or telogen effluvium compared to drug treatment alone over 12 weeks.
Four-weeks daily intake of oral collagen hydrolysate results in improved skin elasticity, especially in sun-exposed areas: a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial
PMID: 32009486 · RCT · positive · oral
Daily oral intake of 5g marine collagen hydrolysate for 4 weeks significantly improved skin elasticity in sun-exposed areas (cheeks) in post-menopausal women, with improvements persisting 4 weeks after discontinuation.
Evaluation of cutaneous rejuvenation associated with the use of ortho-silicic acid stabilized by hydrolyzed marine collagen
PMID: 28941141 · RCT · positive · oral
Oral supplementation of 600 mg ortho-silicic acid stabilized by hydrolyzed marine collagen for 90 days demonstrated statistically significant improvements in skin texture, firmness, and hydration compared to placebo based on clinical evaluation.
Kinetics of moisturizing and firming effects of cosmetic formulations
PMID: 18377622 · clinical_trial · mixed · topical
Marine compound-enriched serums showed superior short-term moisturizing effects while creams performed better with repeated applications, and both formulations produced sustained firming effects during treatment that did not persist after cessation.
