Topical 5-Fluorouracil 5% cream combined with microneedling showed 30-55% clinical improvement in stable vitiligo patients over three months, with 50% achieving moderate-to-excellent response at final session.

PMID: 41566557 · clinical_trial · positive · device
Combined radiofrequency microneedling with 1927 nm thulium laser was superior to RFMN monotherapy for improving pigmentary irregularities and overall skin appearance, while both modalities equally reduced submental volume and improved lower facial and neck laxity without serious adverse events.

PMID: 24280617 · clinical_trial · positive · mixed
