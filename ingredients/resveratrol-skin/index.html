분석된 논문 (8편)

Trans-resveratrol alters mammary promoter hypermethylation in women at increased risk for breast cancer
PMID: 22332908 · RCT · positive · oral
Oral trans-resveratrol (5 or 50 mg twice daily for 12 weeks) decreased methylation of the tumor suppressor gene RASSF-1α in mammary tissue of women at increased breast cancer risk, with changes correlated to serum PGE2 levels.
Efficacy and safety of a topical skincare regimen containing CE Ferulic serum and resveratrol BE serum following ablative fractional CO2 laser treatment: A prospective, randomized, split-face, control
PMID: 38828801 · RCT · positive · topical
A topical regimen combining CE Ferulic and Resveratrol BE serums applied for 14 days post-ablative fractional CO2 laser treatment significantly reduced erythema index, accelerated scab detachment, decreased melanin index by 7.4%, and maintained higher skin hydration compared to normal saline control in a split-face trial.
Efficacy of food supplement to improve metabolic syndrome parameters in patients affected by moderate to severe psoriasis during anti-TNFα treatment
PMID: 24442048 · RCT · positive · oral
Addition of a nutraceutical supplement containing resveratrol (among other ingredients) to etanercept therapy significantly improved HDL cholesterol and triglyceride levels in psoriasis patients with metabolic syndrome compared to etanercept alone.
Trans-resveratrol reduces visible signs of skin ageing in healthy adult females over 40: an 8-week randomized placebo-controlled trial
PMID: 41488277 · RCT · positive · mixed
Combined oral (75mg) and topical (1.5% cream) trans-resveratrol significantly reduced wrinkle scores compared to placebo after 8 weeks, with topical application also increasing sebum levels in healthy females over 40.
Efficacy of liposome-encapsulated 4-n-butylresorcinol and resveratrol cream in the treatment of melasma
PMID: 31347777 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
A cream containing liposome-encapsulated 4-n-butylresorcinol and resveratrol significantly reduced melanin index in melasma lesions as early as week 2, with over half of patients reporting moderate to significant improvement by week 4 and no serious adverse events.
Enhanced skin photoprotection integrating a spray-dried hesperetin nanocapsules semisolid formulation: Development of the formulation and clinical trial study
PMID: 40763396 · clinical_trial · positive · topical
A spray-dried hesperetin nanocapsule formulation with titanium dioxide demonstrated high photoprotective capacity (88%), an SPF of 12.71, and performance comparable to SPF 30 sunscreen in protecting volunteers from UV-induced erythema, transepidermal water loss, and melanin index increases, while showing good safety, stability, and skin adhesion.
A phase I study of muscadine grape skin extract in men with biochemically recurrent prostate cancer: Safety, tolerability, and dose determination
PMID: 26012728 · clinical_trial · mixed · oral
Muscadine grape skin extract (MPX) containing resveratrol at 4,000 mg/day was found to be safe and tolerable in men with biochemically recurrent prostate cancer, with only mild gastrointestinal side effects and a non-significant 5.3-month increase in PSA doubling time.
Inhibition of low-density lipoprotein oxidation by Nagano purple grape (Vitis viniferaxVitis labrusca)
PMID: 20086316 · clinical_trial · positive · oral
Consumption of Nagano Purple grape skins or dried fruits, which contain resveratrol, cyanidin-3-glucoside, and anthocyanidins, significantly inhibited LDL oxidation in human subjects 1 hour after consumption compared to fasting baseline samples.
