Vitamin A-fortified coconut cooking oil significantly improved serum retinol concentrations in Filipino children when combined with other vitamin A-rich foods, demonstrating its effectiveness as a dietary intervention for preventing vitamin A deficiency.

PMID: 26740682 · RCT · negative · oral
